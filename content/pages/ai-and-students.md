# The Temptation

As AI continues to improve it will become increasingly tempting for students to send a prompt to AI, pass it through a 'Humanizer' (an AI tool designed to bypass AI detection software) and turn it in as original work. I have herd many arguments against AI saying 'it cant do this' or 'it hallucinates sources' every argument in that line of thinking will soon be eliminated as machine learning (ML) and large language models (LLM) continue to improve. A deeper root must be found to describe why AI shouldn't do everything for us.

> Human writing has a distinct quality apart from AI simply because it is human, something that machines will never be.
